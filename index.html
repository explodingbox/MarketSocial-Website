The MarketSocial system works around the Facebook page as a unit, customers are able to register, sign in, create & design as many applications as they like, on as many Facebook pages they have. When the customer is happy with their application/s, they are able to activate the amount of Facebook pages required, which are pre-paid amounts of time. One application per Facebook page may be active at a time, though they can be interchanged at any point in time, which means campaigns can be sensitive to the day of the week or the customers landing tab could be changed without having to modify anything inside Facebook.

How to create a Facebook Application with MarketSocial

  1. Add the MarketSocial app to your Facebook page

  2. Click “new app” under your Facebook page.

  3. Use the MarketSocial application designer to create your Facebook application. No coding required, just drag and drop the fields and design elements you wish, and name them according to your data.

  4. Add the MarketSocial Facebook application to your facebook page

  5. Activate your Facebook page!
