The MarketSocial system works around the Facebook page as a unit, you can register, sign in, create & design as many applications as you like, on as many Facebook pages as you want before spending a cent.

When the you are happy with your application/s, simply purchase a period of campaign time for the page you are running your campaign on. One application per Facebook page may be active at a time, though applications can be interchanged at any time, which means campaigns can be sensitive to the day of the week, or you could run many different campaigns over the allocated period of time.

How to create a Facebook Application with MarketSocial

  1. Add the MarketSocial app to your Facebook page

  2. Click “new app” under your Facebook page.

  3. Use the MarketSocial application designer to create your Facebook application. No coding required, just drag and drop the fields and design elements you wish, and name them according to your data.

  4. Add the MarketSocial Facebook application to your facebook page

  5. Activate your Facebook page!
